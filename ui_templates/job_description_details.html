Senior Software Engineer (Full Stack)

tag Job ID: 58291 location_on Singapore corporate_fare Technology & Operations work Full-time

About the Role

As a Senior Full Stack Software Engineer at POSB Bank, you will be part of our Digital Transformation team, building the next generation of banking solutions. You will work on high-availability, mission-critical applications that serve millions of customers across the region. We are looking for an engineer who is passionate about clean code, scalable architecture, and delivering delightful user experiences.

Key Responsibilities

  • check_circle Design and develop scalable, secure, and performant web applications using React and Node.js.
  • check_circle Collaborate with cross-functional teams (Product, Design, DevOps) to define and implement new features.
  • check_circle Write clean, testable, and maintainable code with high unit test coverage.
  • check_circle Lead technical design discussions and perform code reviews to maintain high quality standards.
  • check_circle Optimize applications for speed, performance, and accessibility.

Requirements

  • verified Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
  • verified 5+ years of experience in professional software development.
  • verified Strong proficiency in modern JavaScript/TypeScript, React, and Node.js.
  • verified Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud) and CI/CD pipelines.
  • verified Solid understanding of SQL and NoSQL databases (PostgreSQL, MongoDB).
