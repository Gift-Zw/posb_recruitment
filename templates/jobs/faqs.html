Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about applying for jobs at POSB Bank

How do I apply for a job at POSB?

To apply for a job, first create an account by clicking "Register Profile" on our homepage. Once registered and verified, you can browse available positions, select a job that interests you, and click "Apply Now" to submit your application with your profile and documents.

Do I need to create an account to view job listings?

No, you can browse all open job positions without creating an account. However, you will need to register and verify your email address to submit applications.

What documents do I need to apply?

You'll need to upload your CV/resume. Additional documents such as certificates, cover letters, or portfolios are optional but recommended. Make sure all documents are in PDF format and clearly labeled.

Can I apply for multiple positions?

Yes, you can apply for multiple positions. Each application is independent, and you can reuse your profile information for faster application submission.

How do I verify my email address?

After registering, you'll receive a 6-digit verification code via email. Enter this code on the verification page to activate your account. If you don't receive the code, check your spam folder or request a new one.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a secure link to reset your password. The link expires after 24 hours for security reasons.

Can I update my profile after submitting an application?

Yes, you can update your profile at any time. However, changes to your profile will not affect applications you've already submitted. New applications will use your updated profile information.

Can I reuse my application data?

Yes! When applying for a new position, you can choose to reuse data from your last submitted application, making it faster to apply for multiple jobs.

How long does the application process take?

The application process varies by position. After submitting your application, our HR team will review it. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted via email. You can check the status of your applications in your profile dashboard.

What happens after I submit my application?

After submission, you'll receive a confirmation email. Your application will be reviewed by our HR team. If you're shortlisted, you'll be contacted for the next steps in the recruitment process.

Can I withdraw my application?

Yes, you can view and manage all your applications in your profile dashboard. If you need to withdraw an application, please contact our HR team.

How do I know if I've been shortlisted?

Shortlisted candidates will receive an email notification. You can also check the status of your applications in your profile dashboard, which will show whether you've been shortlisted, rejected, or if your application is still under review.

I'm having trouble uploading documents. What should I do?

Ensure your documents are in PDF format and under 10MB in size. If you continue to experience issues, try using a different browser or clearing your browser cache. Contact support if the problem persists.

I didn't receive my verification code. What should I do?

First, check your spam or junk folder. If you still don't see it, you can request a new verification code. Make sure you've entered the correct email address during registration.

The website isn't loading properly. What can I do?

Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, or use a different browser. We recommend using the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge for the best experience.
